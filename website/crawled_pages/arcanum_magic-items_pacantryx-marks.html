Pacantryx Marks

Pacantryx Marks when unbound appear as a silver flat metallic foil emblem, about two inches across. Each mark binds to a wearer when placed against their body. Once bound, the mark grants the user feats or abilities in accordance with the nature of the mark.

Once bound they can be removed by:

  • an “Uninscribe” action (10 minutes, with the “manipulate” trait), which requires an Arcana check. The GM determines the DC to Uninscribe the item based on its level, rarity, and other circumstances.
  • A successful Dispel Magic (If used upon a target willing to have it removed, the dispel is automatically successful).

Marks that grant feats cannot be counted as satisfying prerequisites for the selection of other feats.

Lesser Marks are Bronze in color rather than silver.

Greater Marks are Gold in color rather than silver.

Mark of the Tiger

Description: Saber-Tooth Tiger Head

Lesser: Once per day this item grants Tiger Swipe I. (Value 25 gp, Level 2)

Moderate: Once per hour this item grants Tiger Swipe II. (Value 150 gp, Level 5)

Greater: Once every 10 minutes this item grants Tiger Swipe III. (Value 1000 gp, Level 10)

Tiger Swipe
Activate f envision; I: When you activate this Mark if your next action is a Trip, apply a +1 item bonus to that Trip attempt. II: +2 bonus III: +3 bonus, and rolled critical failures are treated as failures.

Value: 25gp – 1000 gp

Aura: Enchantment

Level: 2 – 9

Bulk: –

Mark of the Feather

Description: A single downy feather.

While wearing this Mark, you do not trigger traps that use weight or pressure plates as a trigger. Also, treat falls as shorter (see below). This stacks with similar effects, such as Cat Fall.

Lesser: Treat falls as 5 feet shorter. (Value 60 gp, Level 3)

Moderate: Treat falls as 10 feet shorter. (Value 160 gp, Level 6)

Greater: Treat falls as 15 feet shorter. (Value 1000 gp, Level 10)

Aura: Enchantment

Value: 60gp – 1000 gp

Level: 3 – 10

Bulk: –

Mark of the Blade

Description: An ornate short sword.

When using one-handed non-reach weapons in the Sword group, treat those weapons as having the Sweep trait.

Lesser: Once per day this mark grants Blademarked I (Value 25, Level 2)

Moderate: Once per hour this mark grants Blademarked II (Value 160, Level 6)

Greater: Once every 10 minutes this mark grants Blademarked III (Value 1000, Level 10)

Value: 60gp – 1000 gp

Blademarked

Activate f envision;

I: When you activate this Mark gain an untyped +1 bonus to hit and damage on your next strike with a slashing weapon.

II: When you activate this Mark gain an untyped +1 bonus to hit and damage on your next strike with a slashing weapon, and give the weapon the Wounding rune for that attack.

III: When you activate this Mark gain an untyped +2 bonus to hit and damage on your next strike with a slashing weapon, and give the weapon the Wounding rune for that attack.

Aura: Enchantment

Level: 3 – 10

Bulk: –

Mark of Comprehension

Description: Sunrise over an open book.

This mark allows the wearer the ability to cast Comprehend Language as an Arcane spell.  The caster may choose to take an additional action to apply the effects of the Conceal Spell metamagic feat to this casting (as the wizard feat).

Lesser: Once per day this items grants Illuminating Insight I, and the ability to cast Comprehend Language as a level 2 spell. (Value 140 gp, Level 5)

Moderate: Once per hour this items grants Illuminating Insight II, and the ability to cast Comprehend Language as a level 2 spell. (Value 900 gp, Level 10)

Greater: Once every 10 minutes this items grants Illuminating Insight III, and the ability to cast Comprehend Language as a level 2 spell. (Value 5,550 gp, Level 15) 

Value: 60gp – 1000 gp

Illuminating Insight

Activate f envision;

I: When you activate this Mark if your next action is a Recall Knowledge action, add a +1 untyped bonus to that check.

II: When you activate this Mark if your next action is a Recall Knowledge action, add a +2 untyped bonus to that check.

III: When you activate this Mark if your next action is a Recall Knowledge action, add a +3 untyped bonus to that check.

Aura: Enchantment

Level: 3 – 10

Bulk: –

Mark of the Defender

Description: Shield embossed with a dragon’s head.

Lesser: Once per day this mark grants the wearer Swift Shield I. (Value 20 gp, Level 2)

Moderate: Once per hour this mark grants the wearer Swift Shield II. (Value 100 gp, Level 5)

Greater: Once every ten minutes this mark grants the wearer Swift Shield III. (Value 500 gp, Level 9)

Value: 20gp – 500 gp

Illuminating Insight

Activate f envision;

I: When you activate this Mark you may take the Raise a Shield action as a free action.

II: If you Shield Block after having raised your shield using Swift Shield (in the same round), give the shield a +2 status bonus to hardness for this action.

III: If you Shield Block after having raised your shield using Swift Shield (in the same round), give the shield a +4 status bonus to hardness for this action.

Aura: Enchantment

Level: 2 – 9

Bulk: –

Mark of the Snake

Description: Serpent.

Lesser: Once per day this mark grants the wearer Subtle Spell I. (Value 20 gp, Level 2)

Moderate: Once per hour this mark grants the wearer Subtle Spell II. (Value 100 gp, Level 5)

Greater: Once every ten minutes this mark grants the wearer Subtle Spell III. (Value 500 gp, Level 9)

Value: 20 gp – 500 gp

Subtle Spell

Activate f envision;

I: When you activate this Mark you may use the Conceal Spell feat as your next action with a + 1 status bonus to your Stealth check.

II: When you activate this Mark you may use the Conceal Spell feat as your next action with a + 2 status bonus to your Stealth check.

III: When you activate this Mark you may use the Conceal Spell feat as your next action with a + 3 status bonus to your Stealth check.

Aura: Enchantment

Level: 2 – 9

Bulk: –

Mark of Instruction

Description: Owl

Lesser: Once per day this mark grants the wearer Superior Instruction. (Value 20 gp, Level 2)

Moderate: Once per hour this mark grants the wearer Superior Instruction. (Value 100 gp, Level 5)

Greater: Once every ten minutes this mark grants the wearer Superior Instruction. (Value 500 gp, Level 9)

Value: 20 gp – 500 gp

Superior Instruction

Activate f envision;

Trigger: You would provide an ally a circumstance bonus.

Effect: Increase the provided circumstance bonus by 1.

Aura: Enchantment

Level: 2 – 9

Bulk: –
